Book Your Car with Wtours Car
Choose Your Vehicle

Browse our diverse fleet of well-maintained vehicles, ranging from compact cars for city driving to luxury models for a more refined experience. Find the perfect match for your travel needs.

Send Us a Message

Start by sending us a WhatsApp message at +212 7007-79736 . Let us know your name and the type of vehicle you’re interested in.

Provide Booking Details

Share your desired pick-up and drop-off dates, as well as any specific requirements or preferences you have for your rental car.

Receive Vehicle Options

We’ll respond with a selection of available vehicles that match your needs, along with pricing and additional information.

Confirm Your Choice

Once you’ve selected your vehicle, confirm your choice via WhatsApp. We’ll provide you with a summary of your booking details and any further instructions.

Finalize Your Booking

We may request a few additional details to complete your booking. After finalizing, you’ll receive a confirmation message with all the necessary information, including pick-up instructions.

Pick Up Your Vehicle

On the day of your rental, visit our location as per the instructions provided. Our team will ensure a smooth handover of your vehicle and answer any last-minute questions you might have.
